# Client User Guide — GetHeard

**For brands commissioning research on GetHeard.**

Welcome to GetHeard. This guide walks you through everything you need to do — from signing up to receiving your research report.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to **https://getheard.space/listen/signup**
2. Enter your name, company, email, and a password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard

> **Already have an account?** Log in at https://getheard.space/listen/login

---

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your **Client Dashboard** at `/listen`.

Here you'll find:
- **Stats cards** — your active studies, completed studies, and total respondents
- **Your Studies** — a table showing all your commissioned research with current status
- **"Commission New Study"** button — start a new research project

---

## Commissioning a Study (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Brief Your Research

Click **"Commission New Study"** → You'll be taken to a chat interface with our AI research assistant.

The assistant will ask you questions about:
- What you're trying to learn
- Who your target audience is
- Your industry / product
- How many respondents you need
- Which language to interview in
- Any specific topics to cover

**Tips for a good brief:**
- Be specific about your objective (e.g., "Why do users drop off during KYC?" not just "Understand KYC")
- Mention the country/city of your target audience
- If you have specific competitor names or product features, mention them
- You don't need to know everything upfront — the assistant will guide you

The conversation takes about 3–5 minutes. When you're done, click **Confirm Brief** (or the assistant will do it automatically).

---

### Step 2: Review Your Quote

After your brief, you'll see a detailed price breakdown:

| Line Item | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Study Fee | Base price for your study type × panel size multiplier |
| Recruitment Fee | Cost to recruit respondents from our panel |
| Respondent Incentive | Optional: add ₹X per respondent to boost participation |
| Urgency Premium | +25% if you need results in <3 days |
| **Total** | **All costs in INR** |

**You have 3 levers to adjust the price:**

1. **Panel Size** — Slide to choose 5–60 respondents. Fewer = cheaper + faster.
2. **Panel Source** — *GetHeard Panel* (we recruit), *Your List* (you provide CSV), or *Targeted* (custom recruitment for harder-to-reach audiences).
3. **Study Type** — Switch between NPS/CSAT, Feature Feedback, Pain Points, or Custom if needed.

The price updates live as you move the sliders. When you're happy, click **Confirm Quote**.

---

### Step 3: Review Your Timeline

You'll see an estimated delivery timeline broken into phases:

| Phase | What Happens |
|-------|-------------|
| Panel Recruitment | We select respondents matching your criteria |
| Scheduling | Respondents confirm availability |
| Interviews | AI-moderated voice interviews conducted |
| Analysis | AI analyses all transcripts |
| Report Delivery | You receive your report |

**Standard timeline:** 4–7 business days for most studies.

**Need it faster?** Toggle **Urgency** to compress the timeline (+25% fee applies).

When ready, click **Proceed to Payment**.

---

### Step 4: Pay

You can pay by:

**Razorpay** (India & SEA — Credit/Debit card, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets)
1. Click "Pay with Razorpay"
2. A payment popup will appear
3. Choose your payment method
4. Complete payment
5. You'll be redirected to your study status page

**Stripe** (International — Credit/Debit card)
1. Click "Pay with Stripe"
2. You'll be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page
3. Enter your card details
4. On completion, you'll return to your study status

---

### Step 5: Approve Your Panel

After payment, our system builds your respondent panel. You'll receive an email when it's ready.

On your **Study Status** page, you'll see a list of selected respondents showing:
- Name (first name only)
- Age range
- Language
- City
- Gender

Review the list and click **Approve Panel**. If you'd like any changes, contact us at np@dendrons.ai.

Once approved, respondents will receive a WhatsApp message inviting them to their interview.

---

### Step 6: Track Your Study Live

Your **Study Status** page updates every 10 seconds automatically. You'll see:

```
✅ Brief Complete
✅ Quote Confirmed
✅ Payment Received
✅ Panel Built
✅ Panel Approved
🔄 Interviews in Progress (12 of 20 complete)
⏳ Analysis
⏳ Report Ready
```

You don't need to do anything during this phase — just watch the counter go up!

---

### Step 7: Receive Your Report

When all interviews are complete and analysis finishes, you'll receive an **email with your report link**.

Your report includes:
- **Executive Summary** — 2–3 paragraph narrative of key findings
- **Sentiment Overview** — What % of respondents felt positive/neutral/negative
- **Key Themes** — Top recurring themes with frequency counts and verbatim quotes
- **Question-by-Question Insights** — What respondents said to each interview question
- **Pain Points** — Specific friction points with severity ratings
- **Positive Highlights** — What's working well
- **Recommendations** — 5 specific, actionable things to do next

**Sharing your report:**
- Click **"Share Report"** to get a shareable link
- Click **"Download PDF"** to save a copy

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does a typical study take?**
4–7 business days for a 20-respondent study. Urgency option available for 2–4 days.

**Can I provide my own respondents?**
Yes! Select "Your List (CSV)" as the panel source, and we'll send you a CSV template to fill in.

**What languages can you conduct interviews in?**
English, Hindi, Indonesian, Filipino, Thai, Vietnamese, and Mandarin. More languages on request.

**Can I see the raw transcripts?**
Not currently — the report includes verbatim quotes. Full transcript access is on our roadmap.

**Can I have the report in English even if interviews were in Hindi?**
Yes — the AnalysisAgent analyses transcripts and writes the report in English by default, with original-language quotes preserved.

**What if I'm not happy with the respondents?**
Contact us before approving the panel. We'll rebuild it at no extra cost.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. All data is stored on secure servers. Respondent personal details are anonymised in your report (you see age range, not names).

**Can I cancel after paying?**
Please contact us within 24 hours of payment. Cancellations after panel building begins are subject to a recruitment fee.
